About the Author

Meet Ki-Jana Thomas — Father, Author, and the Heart Behind the "And Baby" Series

Ki-Jana Thomas discovered his passion for writing children’s books after the birth of his son, Karter. Becoming a father opened his eyes to the magic of everyday moments — the laughter, the challenges, the milestones — and inspired him to capture those memories through storytelling. That’s how the “And Baby” series was born.

Through books like Black, Beautiful & Baby and Black, Puerto Rican & Baby, Ki-Jana creates heartwarming stories that celebrate identity, culture, and confidence. His mission is to fill the shelves with stories that reflect the beauty of every child — especially those who have been underrepresented for too long.

But these books aren’t just for families of color — they’re for everyone. Ki-Jana believes that through stories, we can all learn to love ourselves and better understand each other. His writing is a bridge — teaching children to embrace who they are while inspiring families to celebrate the uniqueness in others.

Whether it's a bedtime laugh in Go To Sleep, Karter or a lesson in self-love and joy, every Cool, Calm & Karter book is crafted with care, connection, and a big dose of heart.

And the impact doesn’t stop on the page. For every book sold, we donate a copy to a child in foster care, a child in need, or an underserved community — because we believe every child deserves to feel seen, celebrated, and loved.

Thank you for supporting Ki-Jana and the team at Cool, Calm & Karter. With your help, we’re building a brighter, more inclusive future — one book at a time.
